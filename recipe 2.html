Preparation of Nutrient Agar

  1. Ingredients

    • Beef extract (or meat extract): 3.0 g
    • Peptone: 5.0 g
    • Sodium chloride (NaCl): 5.0 g
    • Agar: 15.0 g
    • Distilled water: 1,000 mL

  2. Preparation Steps

    1. Dissolve ingredients
      • Add beef extract, peptone, and NaCl to distilled water
      • Mix until fully dissolved
    2. Add agar
      • Add agar powder to the solution
      • Heat until the agar completely dissolves
    3. Adjust pH
      • Adjust to pH 7.0 ± 0.2 (usually with 1N NaOH or HCl)
    4. Dispense
      • Pour into flasks or bottles (or distribute into test tubes for slants)
    5. Sterilize
      • Autoclave at 121 °C for 15 minutes
    6. Pour plates
      • If making Petri dishes, cool the medium to about 45–50 °C,
        then pour into sterile Petri plates in aseptic conditions
      • Let solidify

    Thus the preparation of nutrient agar is fully complete
